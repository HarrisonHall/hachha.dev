The modern isekai scene has a beautiful quality: each entry is exactly the same.
It's a comfort genre-- each story retells the same plot with one, at most two,
changes/twists that keep it interesting (which are typically outlived after
several episodes). When I discovered my first "villainess" show several years
ago, I thought to myself "this is fairly novel!" Boy, was I mistaken.

_Villainess_ anime (_doom-flag otome_) encompass a series of tropes that make it
a subgenre of the typical isekai:

- [ ] Character is reincarnated from our world into a fantasy world
- [ ] Fantasy world is from a visual novel
- [ ] Character takes on the role of the villainess
- [ ] Character must avoid the game's "doom flags" to prevent demise
- [ ] Avoid these flags:
  - [ ] Rejection from fiance (duke/prince)
  - [ ] Accusation of bullying
- [ ] Other misc. tropes:
  - [ ] Prepare for battle with Demon King
