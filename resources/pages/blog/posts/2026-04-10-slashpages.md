I have updated this site to conform to various
[indieweb slashpages](https://slashpages.net/) specs. Apparently,
[links](/links) page was already conforming!

Many of the pages still need work, but others are fairly complete.
[Colophon](/colophon) has been touched on before with prior blog posts. I had
wanted a sort of "lists" page for a while, which is taken care of with
[carry](/carry), [defaults](/defaults), and [top4](/top4). [TIL](/til) _will_ be
updated, but doesn't have much depth as of now.

I'm looking forward to implementing other specs used in the indieweb (e.g.,
webfinger, webmention). While I support posse, I don't actually care about
advertising anything on this site. Let me know if something doesn't look right.
